What is Full Stack Development?

Full Stack Development Explained

Full Stack Development refers to the development of both front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) portions of a web application. A full stack developer is a versatile professional who can work on all layers of software development, from designing user interfaces to managing databases and server infrastructure.
